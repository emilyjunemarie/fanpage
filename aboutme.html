About Me

My name is Draco Malfoy and I am a wizard. I was born June 5, 1980 to two British wizards, Lucius and Narcissa Malfoy. I grew up attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry where I was placed into the Slytherin House. Growing up as the son of a Death Eater, I was raised on very strict beliefs. Similar to my known rival Harry Potter, many call me "The Chosen One of the Dark Side" due to my leniant obidience to the Dark Lord. I have struggled to find my own courage, but there are wizards out there, as well as muggles, who know who I truly am and what I stand for.
